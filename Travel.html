01

Manali

Manali – Let Your ‘Jawani’ Go ‘Deewani’

Manali is usually the first choice of all college-goers in Northern India. Ease of access, a happening vibe, and picturesque mountains are some of the main attractions that bring the young (and old) to this town. It is among the best places to visit in India with friends

Things To Do In Manali :-

Paragliding, rappelling, trekking, wildlife spotting in the Great Himalayan National Park, river rafting, zorbing, quad biking, and more.

Place To Stay :-

Sterling Manali, Manali Grand

Average Budget :-

INR 4,000 to 6,000

02

Rishikesh

Rishikesh – River Raft Together

Our definition of washing our sins in the Ganges is a little hatke. What we require for it is an inflatable boat, life jacket, carbon paddles, some good brave friends, and probably also a camera!

Things To Do In Rishikesh :-

River rafting, riverside camping, bungee jumping, flying fox, and more.

Place To Stay :-

Aloha On The Ganges, Rishikesh, Swiss Cottage and Spa By Salvus, Rishikesh

Average Budget :-

INR 10,000 to 13,000

03

Goa

Goa – Because ‘Dil Chahta Hai’

We’ve all watched ‘Dil Chahta Hai’ over and over again for the same thing – that perfect Goa trip with friends which most of us still only dream of. Goa is one of the best places in India to visit with friends.

Things To Do In Goa :-

Watersports, crab catching, exploring the nightlife, dolphin tours, cruising, island hopping, and more.

Place To Stay :-

DoubleTree by Hilton Goa – Panaji, Grand Hyatt Goa

Average Budget :-

INR 5,000 to 8,000

04

Ladakh

Ladakh – Go On The Chadar Trek

While there are rivers that allow you to raft in, there are some you can even walk on. Confused? The river Zanskar is one of the best places to visit in India with friends that freezes in the months of January and December

Things To Do In Ladakh :-

Trekking, Sightseeing.

Place To Stay :-

The Grand Dragon Ladakh, The Indus Valley

Average Budget :-

INR 8,000 to 11,000

05

Pondi

Pondicherry – An All-Girls Vacay

A firm believer of ‘Sisters Before Misters’? Then Pondicherry is your playground ladies! If you are looking for some exciting, yet absolutely safe places to visit with your girlfriends, then there’s no better option than this!

Things To Do In Pondicherry :-

Scuba diving, boating, watersports, sunbathing, bicycle tours, explore the nightlife, & more.

Place To Stay :-

Hotel Bon Sejour, Hotel Atithi

Average Budget :-

INR 5,000 to 8,000

06

Nainital

Nainital – Go On A Boating Adventure

Known as the Lake district of India, the top attraction here is the Naini Lake where boating is one of the best things to do along with a horseback ride to the top of Naina Peak, the highest point.

Things to do in Nainital :-

Boating, Hiking, bird-watching, nature trails.

Place To Stay :-

The Naini Retreat, Nainital, Hotel Chevron Fairhavens, Nainital

Average Budget :-

INR 10,000 to 13,000

07

Hampi

Hampi – For The History-Lovers

If your take on a ‘friends trip’ involves exploring ancient ruins of prestigious temples and antiquated monuments that have secrets lurking in their very foundations, then you really need a trip to Hampi.

Things To Do In Hampi :-

Rowing coracles, biking, bathing with elephants, cliff jumping, scaling paddy fields, and more.

Place To Stay :-

Kishkinda Heritage Resort, Love Hampi Homestay

Average Budget :-

INR 5,000 to 8,000
